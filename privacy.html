Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: March 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use our contact form or book a consultation, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and details about the services you're interested in. We also collect anonymous analytics data through Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and consultation requests
  • Provide childcare consulting services
  • Send follow-up communications related to your consultation
  • Improve our website and services

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies & Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about website usage, including pages visited, time spent, and referral sources. Google Analytics uses cookies to track this information. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On. We also store a cookie to remember your cookie consent preference.

4. Data Storage & Security

Contact form submissions are transmitted securely via our email provider (Mailgun). We do not store your form data in a database. Email correspondence is retained in our business email account and protected with industry-standard security measures.

5. Your Rights Under PIPEDA

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for the collection or use of your data

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — anonymous website usage tracking
  • Mailgun — email delivery for contact form submissions
  • Google Fonts — web font delivery

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.
